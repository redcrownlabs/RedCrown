This release makes watched-state management explicit and keeps discovery
focused on content you have not finished.

## Highlights

- Mark movies or one selected series episode as watched from the title page.
- Continue Watching opens the exact first playable regular episode that is not
  marked watched, including episodes added after you caught up.
- Right-click media cards on Home, Movies, Series, Anime, My Library, or title
  artwork for consistent watched actions.
- Mark all currently known regular episodes of a series as watched from its card
  context menu; per-episode control remains available on the title page.
- Remove a series from Continue Watching without deleting its watched history,
  and restore it later from the same context menu.
- Hide watched movies globally from Home and movie discovery. The preference is
  enabled by default and can be changed under **Settings → Discovery**.

## Catalog and title details

- Torrent choices show their download size when supplied by the catalog.
- Hovering or focusing a torrent choice reveals its filename when available.
- Popcorn Time watched-series imports retain available title, year, and poster
  metadata so Continue Watching can identify imported shows.

## Fixes

- Marking or unmarking one series episode no longer changes sibling episodes.
- Continue Watching no longer treats the highest watched episode as proof that
  every earlier episode was watched.
- Playback cancel and back controls now remain fully clickable above the custom
  Electron window drag region, including their text labels.
- Watched-movie filtering is presentation-only and never removes catalog or
  library records.

## Upgrade notes

- Existing library databases migrate automatically. Watched history, favorites,
  and imported records are preserved.
- Existing settings files receive **Hide watched movies** enabled by default to
  preserve the prior Home behavior.
- RedCrown still requires at least one user-configured compatible catalog API.
- Windows builds remain unsigned pre-releases. Verify downloads with the
  attached `SHA256SUMS.txt` file.
